# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Give constructive, actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Help newcomers learn and contribute.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Abusive language or threats.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that blocks collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately with details.
